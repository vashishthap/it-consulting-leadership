About Nothing But Value

Nothing But Value was founded on a simple premise: SME IT services companies deserve the same quality AI transformation expertise as Fortune 500 enterprises—without the Fortune 500 price tag.

Our Story

After leading AI transformations at some of the world's largest companies, our founders recognized a massive gap in the market. While big consulting firms like McKinsey, Deloitte, and Accenture charge $500K-$2M+ for AI transformation projects, SMEs with 50-250 employees were left behind—unable to afford enterprise consulting but desperately needing AI expertise to stay competitive.

We decided to change that. Nothing But Value brings Fortune 500-caliber AI executives directly to SME IT services companies at 1/5th the cost.

Our Approach: The 70-20-10 Framework

Unlike AI developers who focus purely on technology, we understand that successful AI transformation is about people first:

70% People & Change Management: Training, adoption, culture shift, and stakeholder alignment

20% Data & Architecture: Data readiness, governance frameworks, and infrastructure design

10% AI Models & Technology: Selecting and implementing the right AI solutions

This is why 95% of AI projects fail—they focus on the 10% (technology) and ignore the 90% (people and data). We get it right.
