Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How are you different from Big 4 consulting firms? +
We're former Fortune 500 AI executives who've actually built and scaled AI systems. Unlike Big 4 firms that charge $500K-$2M and take 12-18 months, we deliver production-ready solutions in 6-8 weeks for $50K-$200K. We don't send junior consultants - you work directly with senior AI leaders. And we don't just create PowerPoints - we build, deploy, and train your team.
What if we don't have clean data? +
Most companies don't have perfect data - that's normal. We start with a data readiness assessment and help you clean and structure your data as part of the engagement. We use modern AI techniques that can work with imperfect data, and we prioritize quick wins that don't require extensive data preparation. Data quality improves iteratively as we implement solutions.
How quickly can we see ROI? +
Our Quick-Win Pilot program delivers measurable ROI in 6-8 weeks. We focus on high-impact, low-complexity use cases first to prove value quickly. For example, an AI chatbot for customer support can reduce ticket volume by 40-60% within the first month of deployment. Larger transformations show ROI within 3-6 months.
Do we need to hire AI specialists? +
Not initially. We handle the heavy lifting and train your existing team to manage and optimize the AI solutions. For Quick-Win Pilots, your current IT team can manage the systems after handover. For larger transformations, we help you hire the right AI talent when needed, but you don't need specialists on day one.
What industries do you work with? +
We work with SME businesses (50-500 employees) across multiple industries including IT Services (MSPs, software development, consulting), Professional Services (legal, accounting, consulting), Manufacturing, Healthcare, Financial Services, and Retail. While we have deep expertise in IT, our AI frameworks and methodologies apply across industries. What's included in the AI Readiness Audit? +
The audit includes: (1) Assessment across 5 pillars (Strategy, Data, Technology, People, Governance), (2) Gap analysis vs industry leaders, (3) Prioritized roadmap with ROI projections for each use case, (4) Quick-win opportunities you can implement immediately, (5) Risk assessment and mitigation plan, (6) Budget and timeline estimates. Delivered in 2-3 weeks for $10K-$20K.
Do you offer ongoing support after implementation? +
Yes. Every engagement includes a handover period where we train your team and document everything. After that, you can choose: (1) Self-manage with our documentation and training, (2) Fractional AI Leadership for ongoing strategic guidance ($10K-$20K/month), or (3) Project-based support for new initiatives.
